Savy is a technology company that drives data-driven pricing decisions for thousands of businesses around the world. We seamlessly integrate into retailers of every size, resulting in profit centric strategies that have proven to promote brand value and preserve product margins.

right price = maximum profits
The fastest and most effective way for a company to realize maximum profits is to get its pricing right. The right price can boost profits faster than any other method; the wrong price can shrink them just as quickly.

The most substantial opportunity to increase revenue is to leverage price optimization at a transaction to transaction basis. But managing and understanding pricing on a microscopic level is nearly impossible due to the complexity and volume of transactions in high growth businesses.

This is where Savy strives to add value. We take care of price optimization on a transactional level so that you don’t have to.
